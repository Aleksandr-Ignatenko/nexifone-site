Text-to-Speech

Enterprise-grade Text-to-Speech delivery for telecom and business messaging.

Text-to-Speech (TTS) converts an SMS message into a voice call in near real-time. This approach significantly improves message reach and delivery reliability.

A text message is submitted to the Nexifone network using standard telecom protocols. The platform automatically converts the text into a voice call and delivers it to the end user.

The recipient receives a call from a registered clean number and hears the message read aloud. Before playback, a short announcement informs the user that the message was converted from SMS.

Why Use Text-to-Speech

  • Seamless integration into existing SMS routing
  • Pay only for successfully delivered voice messages
  • Recover undelivered SMS traffic automatically
  • No brand or campaign registration required
  • Secure delivery for sensitive notifications
  • Additional service for your SMS portfolio
  • CNAM support for improved brand visibility

How to Connect

Connecting to Nexifone Text-to-Speech is identical to standard SMS connectivity. Traffic can be enabled instantly without operational changes.

  • SMPP (username and password)
  • HTTP API
  • Campaign & Traffic Management Portal

Nexifone operates a modern, carrier-grade platform designed for stability, scalability, and fast onboarding.

Supported Languages

Nexifone Text-to-Speech supports a wide range of languages, including:

Arabic, Chinese (Mandarin), Danish, Dutch, English (US, UK, AU, IN, Welsh), French (including Canadian), German, Hindi, Icelandic, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese (including Brazilian), Romanian, Russian, Spanish, Swedish, Turkish.

Nexifone focuses on reliability, transparency, and long-term partnerships. Text-to-Speech termination is delivered using proprietary routes and technology.
